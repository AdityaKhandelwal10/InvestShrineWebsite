About

We're an entrepreneurship platform, and our goal is to make entrepreneurial funding available to the masses, by the masses.
And no, we are not a crowdfunding platform. We allow people to invest small amounts in new ventures which may not require millions of dollars,
and hence giving them access to these startups which may become the next big thing! And if you're an entrepreneur who does not require
millions of dollars, and don't want to go through the hassle of VC firms, this is the right place for you! Avail funding through investors
who will sign up on our platform, and get your journey started!

The main problem:

A lot of entrepreneurs in your locality/city might be working on their idea, even if it's not the Apple of 2020, it could be the Apple of 1977.
You, as an entrepreneur, may not require a lot of money right now, and might want to get your funding quick. While VC firms are great,
you can't deny the amount of time you're going to have to put in to get an interview with them. You can sign up on our platform, have your
venture verified by us, and we'll give you access to the common citizens who are willing to fund you.
As an investor, you might already be trading in the stock market, but this is a different thing altogether. This platform will give you access
to these amazing ventures in their early stages; and by investing in them, giving yourself the opportunity to reap profits for a lifetime.
